About Dr. Nguyen Van A

Dr. Nguyen Van A is a highly experienced cardiologist with over 15 years of practice in cardiovascular medicine. He specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of heart diseases, hypertension, and cardiac arrhythmias.

Dr. Nguyen completed his medical degree at Ho Chi Minh City University of Medicine and Pharmacy, followed by specialized training in cardiology at the National Heart Institute. He has published numerous research papers in international medical journals and is recognized as one of the leading cardiologists in Vietnam.

He is known for his compassionate approach to patient care and his expertise in both invasive and non-invasive cardiac procedures.

Education & Qualifications
  • MD - Ho Chi Minh City University of Medicine and Pharmacy (2008)
  • Specialization in Cardiology - National Heart Institute (2012)
  • Fellowship in Interventional Cardiology - Singapore General Hospital (2015)
  • Board Certified in Cardiovascular Medicine
  • Member of Vietnam Cardiology Association
